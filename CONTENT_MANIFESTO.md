*   **BWorth Coins**: 1 Coin = ₹1 value. Earn instantly by selling unused clothes.
*   **The Mission**: Revolutionizing the fashion industry through a unique buyback program that preserves the planet's beauty.
*   **The Ecosystem**: Repurposing fashion through reuse, upcycling, and recycling to eliminate landfill waste.
